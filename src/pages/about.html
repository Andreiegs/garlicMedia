About Us

Driven by a passion for design, Ariel A. Pacheco, the owner of the company, drew upon his graphic design skills to launch Garlic Media, an online t-shirt printing business in Laoag City, Ilocos Norte. In addition to t-shirt printing, the company also offers sticker printing services and caters its products to customers seeking custom graphic design solutions.
